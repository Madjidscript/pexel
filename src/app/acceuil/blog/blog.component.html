Overexposed Podcast S1 E1: Kishan Mistry (Photography & Videography)

Kishan Mistry is a photographer, videographer, and designer based out of Toronto, Ontario. Kishan got his start early on in his high school days playing around with all different forms and mediums of media. His love for “hoops & music” lead him to have worked with the likes of Drake, Migos, Travis Scott, and the Toronto Raptors. In this episode, we talk about the importance of 'the hustle' when it comes to pursuing a creative passion.
